I’m Nicolás Bronzina, an Argentinean Design Researcher living in Madrid

The website is programmed using HTML code from a text editor on my Mac computer, based on static sites.

[EN] Social Scientist and Researcher with a Master's in User Experience Design.

As a Design Researcher, I focus on enriching and advancing the speculative aspects of design through extensive research, diegetic prototyping, and translating conceptual frameworks into tangible ideas. My work encompasses the future of food, urban environments, ecology, and emerging technologies.

In addition to being a managing partner at BPP and contributing to design fiction writing, I'm a regular contributor to Girardin & Nova. There, I'm dedicated to in-depth research on emerging trends and 'weak signals' to identify possible future scenarios inside and outside organizations.

Projects

San Telmo Market

A Design Fiction work that explores the City of Buenos Aires's potential future and gastronomy through the evolution of one of its iconic markets, the San Telmo Market. It shows the market's different stalls, shops, and services influenced by long periods of extreme heat and droughts.
