Nicolás Bronzina

—AI-Enhanced Futures Designer

Buenos Aires ↔ Madrid

Your organization navigates uncertain futures. I build prototypes and design fiction artifacts that turn research into decisions you can actually act on.

Through BPP Analytics & Design, we combine research, futures design, and experimentation to support high-stakes strategic choices. I designed the Emerging Technologies course for UDIT Madrid’s Online Master’s in UX Design, and run workshops for design teams working with AI and futures methods. At Heated Studio, I help organizations imagine and prototype new climate futures.

Previously at Near Future Laboratory and Girardin & Nova, I’ve delivered design fiction projects for global clients across telecommunications, finance, defense, and mobility — turning abstract risk into artifacts teams can argue about.

I document futures design methods and experiments through Otros Futuros, a YouTube channel where speculative thinking becomes tangible practice. I also publish MARGIN.SIGNALS, an independent publication tracking early signals from cultural margins before they harden into mainstream trends.
