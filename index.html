[EN] Social Scientist and Researcher with a Master's in User Experience Design.

As a Design Researcher, I'm dedicated to enriching and advancing the speculative aspects of design through a multi-faceted approach, including extensive research, diegetic prototyping, and translation of conceptual frameworks into tangible ideas. My work is grounded in the future of food, but I'm also interested in other topics such as new urban environments, ecology, and emerging technologies.

In addition to being a managing partner at BPP and contributing to design fiction writing, I'm a regular contributor to Girardin & Nova. There, I'm dedicated to in-depth research on emerging trends and 'weak signals,' to identify possible future scenarios inside and outside organizations.

San Telmo Market

A Design Fiction work that explores the potential future of the City of Buenos Aires and its gastronomy through the evolution of one of its iconic markets, the San Telmo Market. It shows the market's different stalls, shops, and services influenced by long periods of extreme heat and droughts. A work led by Nicolás Bronzina in collaboration with Fabien Girardin and Israel Viadest at Girardin & Nova. Futures in Maps, an atlas that tells stories about a world we all might experience soon.
