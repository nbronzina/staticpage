Our Purpose

At OFFicial, we are a vital ally for people who, forced to leave behind their homes, lives, and everything they know, face precarious conditions upon arriving in a new country. In an interconnected and technologically advanced world, our mission is to support and empower these individuals in their constant pursuit of a dignified and secure life.

What do we do?
We use innovation and prospective design to create future-oriented artifacts that promote collective reflection and drive social change. We transform the complex realities faced by refugees, projecting effective solutions for the near future. Our approach focuses on:

  • Simplification: We significantly reduce the processing times for asylum applications through deeply empathetic and human interactions.
  • Automation: We alleviate the administrative burden on institutions and streamline processes by integrating advanced intelligent technologies.
  • Facilitating Integration: We build bridges of support that enable agile and meaningful integration into host communities, providing services that interpret and assist refugees at every step.
  • Building Trust: In a constantly changing global environment, we offer clarity and eliminate uncertainty for refugees, ensuring a secure and understandable experience.

How do we do it?
Our most innovative solution is Welcome: a Voice Conversational Assistant powered by advanced Artificial Intelligence. Welcome:

  • Collects and manages data that validate the integration and adaptation of asylum seekers, using this data to continuously improve the user experience.
  • Provides an interactive, personalized voice guide that simplifies the adaptation process, helping refugees navigate and connect with essential services in their new environment.

Welcome embodies our vision of technology that not only solves problems but does so in a human, accessible, and deeply empathetic way. At OFFicial, we remain committed to designing more just and equitable futures, where everyone has the opportunity to rebuild their lives with dignity.

Design Principles

  • The future is about stories of change built in the present: we aim to contribute to a more sustainable and supportive society.
  • We are optimistic, not utopian.
  • We design from real data and for everyone, leaving no one behind.
  • Technology is a tool to solve problems, not a place to spend time.
  • Trust grows with transparency. We simplify processes to make them accessible.
  • We design for a before and after impact.
  • If we believe something is possible, we strive to change it and calmly accept what we cannot change.
  • What is important to our users is also important to our organization.
