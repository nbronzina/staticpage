Our Purpose

At OFFicial, we believe in being a vital ally for people forced to leave behind their homes, lives, and everything they know, often facing precarious conditions upon arriving in a new country. We understand that the journey to stability can be filled with uncertainty, and our mission is to make that journey smoother and more predictable.

What Do We Do?

We use innovation and prospective design to create future-oriented artifacts that promote collective reflection and drive social change. Our work focuses on transforming refugees' complex realities into effective solutions for the near future.

Look & Feel

The development of the graphic elements that make up Welcome is based on two fundamental guidelines: the design principles by which OFFicial operates and the work developed by Syrian graphic designer and refugee Yara Said. In 2015, one of the largest migration crises in history unfolded, with the civil war in Syria, extreme poverty in Niger, and political persecution in Afghanistan and Iraq.

OFFicial App

The OFFicial App is a comprehensive, user-friendly tool that manages asylum seekers' integration and documentation processes. Key features include:

  • Welcome: Quick onboarding with personal profile setup using voice commands.
  • Virtual Documentation Wallet: Securely stores and organizes important documents for easy access.
  • Deadline Calendar: Keeps track of important dates with automatic reminders for document submissions.
  • Access to Your Lawyer: Provides virtual legal support and real-time assistance.
  • Available Resources Directory: A guide to accessible services and support.
  • Local Glossary: A linguistic resource that includes useful local terms and phrases.
  • Location and Maps Access: Provides geolocated information and detailed city maps.
  • Augmented Reality (AR): Helps users find resources intuitively within the app interface.
  • Anonymous Data Collection: Collects data anonymously while fully respecting user privacy.

Biometric Data Wristband

Our complementary wristband provides advanced security and health functions, including biometric sensors, an NFC chip for secure identification, and seamless integration with the OFFicial App. It enhances the experience by ensuring that the most critical information is always easily accessible.
